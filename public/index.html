"Everyday I see or hear something that more or less kills me with delight" - Mary Oliver

This Humid House was founded in 2017 and comprises a team of designers with offices in Singapore and Paris. Our intention is to develop a language expressed through plants and flowers that is reflective of our climate, geography and collective culture.

As a botanical design studio, our focus is on the contemporary. We ask ourselves: how do we put together living material in a way that feels fresh (and relevant) today?
