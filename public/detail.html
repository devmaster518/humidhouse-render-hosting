goshá weekly

goshá to your home i.e., a weekly selection of curated fresh stems delivered to your home, retail space or office. each week you will receive a love letter from goshá in a carefully selected designer vase or in craft wrapping paper for you to place in your own vases.

each goshá designer creation is always unique using your selected reference style as inspiration. our team will capture the feel and composition of your chosen reference using the available freshest seasonal florals, with the signature goshá touch.
